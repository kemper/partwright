# Rubric — makeup (Marked attachment mode, in-code paint)

Score the candidate's makeup against the reference. Each bullet is one judged
item; the judge must say whether it matches and, if not, the specific fix. (This
case is about the painted COLOUR regions, so judge colour placement, not added
geometry.)

- Blush reads as a soft rounded patch on the apple of each cheek (below and beside the eye), pink/rosy
- Blush stays ON the cheeks — it does NOT streak diagonally toward the ears or bleed onto the jaw/side of the face
- Lipstick colours the lips a distinct red/pink, contained to the lip shape (no bleed onto the surrounding skin)
- Eyeshadow tints the upper lid above each eye a mauve/taupe, present but not covering the brow or forehead
- Blush, lipstick, and eyeshadow are all symmetric left-to-right and read as deliberate makeup, not random blotches

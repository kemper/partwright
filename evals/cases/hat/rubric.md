# Rubric — wide-brim hat (Crowned attachment mode)

Score the candidate's hat against the reference. Each bullet is one judged item;
the judge must say whether it matches and, if not, the specific geometry fix.

- A wide, roughly circular brim extends clearly beyond the head all the way around
- A distinct crown rises above the brim with a domed/closed top (not a hollow tube and not an inverted funnel that flares OUT toward the top)
- A hatband reads where the crown meets the brim
- The hat sits ON TOP of the head/hair and welds to it as one piece (not floating above, not swallowing the whole head/face)
- From the front the face below the brim stays visible (the brim doesn't cover the eyes/face)
- Brim and crown are chunky enough to print (no paper-thin brim edge)

Negative-P · live
The team's favorite — a bold geometric P knocked clean out of a solid teal rounded-square tile, the bowl's counter left as a floating teal island — now brought to life. The exact die-cut identity is the resting frame; layered over it runs one classy ~5s loop: the tile gives a gentle iso tilt so the cut P reads as a real beveled pocket with lit walls, a soft sheen sweeps diagonally across the teal, and on load the pocket's lit edge settles into place then breathes. Favicon, monochrome, and on-light stay perfectly static.

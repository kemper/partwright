Gyroid
Show off what no competitor's logo can: Partwright generates gyroid / TPMS implicit surfaces. The mark is one patch of a gyroid cell — interlocking S- and Z-strands weaving strictly over-and-under through a checkerboard of saddle points, the unmistakable double-curved signature of the surface — set inside a tidy rounded boundary so it reads as one confident emblem, not noise. Lit teal crests and deep emerald troughs give every strand real round-section dimension, and a very slow phase-flow lets the level-set undulate (the resting frame is already complete). At 16 px it collapses to a bold S-over-Z weave that still unmistakably reads as a gyroid.

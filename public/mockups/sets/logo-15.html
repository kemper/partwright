Impossible part
An impossible tri-bar — three chunky isometric beams chained into a Penrose loop your eye can't quite resolve. Two teal face-shades give the beams real volume; the amber beam is the one that "shouldn't connect," carrying the aha. It reads as a machined solid the renderer almost printed, not a flat optical trick — and the triangular counter it encloses snaps into a confident P (left beam = stem, loop = bowl), sharpest right when it shrinks to a 16 px favicon.

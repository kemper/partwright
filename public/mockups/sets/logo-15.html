Impossible part
An impossible tri-bar built from three chunky isometric beams that loop into a part your eye can't resolve — a Penrose object engineered, not just drawn. Two teal face-shades give the beams real volume; the amber beam is the one that "shouldn't connect," carrying the aha. The hollow triangle the loop encloses reads as a confident counter — the bowl of a P standing on its left beam. It looks like a machined solid that the renderer almost printed, and it simplifies cleanly to a bold iso-beam silhouette at 16 px.

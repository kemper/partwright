Spur gear · print
The spur gear is one of the oldest and most fundamental mechanical forms — a disc ringed with precise rectangular teeth that has transferred rotational force since antiquity, from water mills to clockwork to modern transmissions. Here it prints itself: each FDM layer deposits tooth-by-tooth, building the disc body up from the plate while the deposition glow traces the live edge, until the finished precision part holds complete in teal on its dark build surface.

Iso Part — refined
The team-favorite isometric solid, given real character. Not a plain cube but a machined component: a wide base slab carrying a stepped boss, bored clean through the top. Three teal face-shades light it top / left / right, and a single warm amber edge catches the highest leading corner like a rim-light. The bore + step still read at 32px; at 16px it falls back to a crisp stepped silhouette with a dark bore. A maker's part, dimensional and confident.

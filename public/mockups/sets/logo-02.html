Negative-space tile
App-icon-first. The whole identity is one solid teal rounded-square tile — and the mark is what's cut clean out of it. A bold geometric P is knocked through the tile to the bare canvas; the counter of the bowl stays as a floating teal island, so the cut reads at once as the initial of Partwright and as a precision part with a bored pocket. Pure die-cut shapes, no strokes and no gradients fighting the silhouette — confident at 230px and still crisp at 16. On light, the tile inverts to deep navy with the same P cut to pale.

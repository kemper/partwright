Hex bolt · print
The hex socket head cap screw — the Allen bolt — is the fundamental fastener of precision manufacturing: a cylindrical shaft topped by a wider cap with a recessed hex drive, made to be torqued exact. The primary mark shows one printing itself FDM-style on the dark iso build plate, shaft depositing layer by layer from the ground up before the wider socket head caps it, layer by layer. A single amber catch-light kisses the top leading edge of the head — the maker's spark, the moment the part is done.

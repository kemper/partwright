The same P pocket bored into a teal block, but lit from the side like a part just pulled from the mill and held to a raking shop light. Oblique illumination floods the left face and every left-facing pocket wall in bright teal while the right side drops into deep shadow — every machined wall jumps out in sharp contrast as though the cutter passed just moments ago. The raised boss inside the counter gets the same side-lit treatment, catching the light from its left cheek.

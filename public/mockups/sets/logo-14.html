Voronoi shell
A bold letter P, its solid skin replaced by a parametric Voronoi lattice — the exact generative infill Partwright stitches into a part on the way to the printer. Bright teal cell-walls web across a dark navy interior; a handful of cells are flooded with translucent teal so the shell reads as a real, dimensional solid rather than a flat outline. The packing tightens toward the letter's edges, the way an FEM-driven infill stiffens a wall. At 16 px the web drops away to a confident P silhouette holding just four fat cells — still unmistakably a latticed part.

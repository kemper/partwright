Impossible P · loop
The P's bowl as an impossible closed frame: four teal beams locked into a rectangular loop where the top bar passes in front of the stem at the upper junction and the bottom bar passes behind it at the lower one. One letter, two lies — the contradiction runs silently through both corners simultaneously, pure teal-on-teal, no extra parts needed to carry the trick.

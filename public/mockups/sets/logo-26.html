Hex standoff · print
A standoff is the humble hero of any assembled device — the piece that holds everything at exactly the right distance. The mark is one printing itself hex-face by hex-face, knurled and precise: a short stout hexagonal column with a cross-hatched waist and a dark threaded bore on top, building up layer by layer from the plate and looping. The resting frame is the finished standoff, solid and ready.
